Nate Bennett says: The Energy Information Administration, a subsidiary of the Department of Energy, keeps track of the average weekly price for a gallon of gas in many major metropolitan areas in the US, and they also keep track of the average weekly spot price for a barrel of crude oil. The price of gasoline has been used to predict the traffic flows on the major freeways in LA County, thus a model that predicts this price would be of use to economists.
